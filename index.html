Frequently asked questions

Q1

What does Fhixa offer in terms of software development services?

Fhixa specializes in custom software development tailored to your unique business needs. Our experienced team of developers creates efficient and secure software solutions designed to drive your business forward.

Q2

Tell us more about your security penetration testing services

We provide two days of free security penetration testing for each software product we develop. During this period, our top security web penetration researcher, ranked 12th worldwide in Facebook's bug bounty hunter list, will thoroughly test the software. This ensures it meets the highest security standards.

Q3

What if I'm satisfied with the free security testing?

If you're satisfied with the results of the free security testing, we offer paid plans for ongoing and more comprehensive penetration testing to ensure your software remains secure over time.

Q4

How can Fhixa help my business grow rapidly?

Our dynamic team of developers and cybersecurity specialists is dedicated to propelling your business growth. We provide tailored software solutions that are not only efficient but also highly secure, ensuring your business operations run smoothly while safeguarding your data.

Q5

Can you provide examples of your team's achievements in cybersecurity?

Our top security web penetration researcher has an impressive track record, including discovering vulnerabilities in Facebook, Indeed, and being ranked 12th worldwide as of 2023 in Facebook's bug bounty hunter list. These achievements highlight our team's exceptional skills and commitment to cybersecurity.

Q6

What sets Fhixa apart from other software development and cybersecurity firms?

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to both software development and cybersecurity. We combine the two seamlessly, creating efficient and secure solutions that help businesses not only meet industry standards but set new benchmarks for success. Our team's expertise and achievements in cybersecurity further reinforce our dedication to your data's safety.
